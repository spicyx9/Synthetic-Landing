Frequently asked questions

Is this compliant with LinkedIn's Terms of Service?

No third-party tool can call itself "LinkedIn-compliant". LinkedIn's User Agreement discourages all forms of automation, including manual schedulers. What we do: stay within LinkedIn's published daily activity limits, only act on people who engaged with your content, and never mass-scrape. We encourage every user to review and comply with LinkedIn's User Agreement. Responsibility for your account stays with you.

How is this different from other LinkedIn automation tools?

Most tools in this category are built for cold outreach. DMing strangers at scale. Synthetic Swarm only acts on people who already engaged with you. They commented. They opted in. That's the difference between starting a conversation and interrupting one.

What if my post only has 5 comments? Or 500?

Both work. No minimum, no maximum. We iterate through everyone who commented, whether that's 5 people or 5,000. High-volume posts get spread across multiple days to stay within your account's daily activity limits.

How long does setup take?

About 3 minutes. Connect your LinkedIn, paste your post URL, write your DM, ship it. No tracking pixels, no CRM integration, no Zapier hell.

What if someone doesn't reply to my first DM?

Synthetic Swarm automatically sends 2-3 follow-ups spaced over several days. You write them once, they trigger only on no-reply, and they stop the moment someone responds.

Does this work for multiple LinkedIn accounts?

Yes. You can connect multiple LinkedIn accounts under one workspace. Useful if you run a personal brand and a company page, or if you're an agency operating LinkedIn for clients.
